I-Chun (Arthur) Liu

I am a first-year master's student in computer science at the University of Southern California (USC). I am working as a research assistant in the Robotics Embedded Systems Laboratory, advised by Professor Gaurav Sukhatme. My research interests are in deep reinforcement learning, vision, and sim-to-real transfer for robotics.

Before attending USC, I graduated from the University of Wisconsin-Madison with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science and the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) with a Master of Science in Computer Science.
